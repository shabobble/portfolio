About Me

I've been interested in coding since a young age, when my grandfather introduced me to HTML4. I went to computer camps for five summers in junior high and high school, with Ace Computer Camps for one summer and CyberCamps after that, taking pretty much every course they had on offer at the time from HTML to Flash animation. I took every computer-related course that my high school offered while I was there, and worked with one of the computer teachers in my senior year on an open study elective project to build my own website. I fell off a bit in college, but am looking to get back on the horse and learn as much as I can of what I missed in the intervening years so I can pursue a career I can be passionate about.
